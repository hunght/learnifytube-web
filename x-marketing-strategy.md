**Don't just shout. Mingle.**

### 1. The "Helpful Reply"
- Search for: "download youtube videos", "youtube distractions", "best way to take notes on videos".
- **Reply:** "Hey! If you're looking to focus, I built an open-source tool called LearnifyTube that removes ads and lets you take notes. Might help! [Link]"

- When you curate content (Pillar 2), tag the creators.
- "Just watched an amazing tutorial by @TraversyMedia. Used LearnifyTube to take notes on the key React hooks. Highly recommend his channel!"
- **Why:** Creators might retweet to their audience.

### 3. Smart Lists
- Create public lists: "Best Coding Educators", "Study Motivation".
- Add people to them. They get a notification. It puts you on their radar.
